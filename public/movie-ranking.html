The Best Movies According To Nelson

Top 3 Greatest of All Time

The Jacket

Captivating audiences with its unique blend of psychological drama and supernatural elements, in "The Jacket," a Gulf War veteran is subjected to an experimental treatment that sends him on a mind-bending journey through time. Adorned with stellar performances and a thought-provoking narrative, the film seamlessly weaves a gripping tale of identity, trauma, and the thin line between sanity and the surreal.

The Exorsist

This movie is a classic. It follows a priest trying do an exorcism ritual to get a demon out of a possessed little girl. The movie is considered one of the scariest movies of all time, and recently a new Exorsist movie (Exorcist Believer) is now out.

The Godfather

Chronicling the life of Don Vito Corleone and his mafia family; the film features the harsh reality of crime life around the time when the italian mob had great influence and control in the United States. The film is regarded as one of the best movies of all time by many worldwide.
